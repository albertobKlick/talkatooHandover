Talkatoo AI Assistant

Give those unwanted tasks to your Assistant.

Talkatoo assistant lives on your desktop and helps you streamline veterinary administrative tasks with your voice! Ask for things like:

Transcribe and Summarize Client Calls
Summarize the follow-up call we just recorded with Jamie Rogers about his dog Fluffy. Please make it no longer than 250 words.
Provide Dosage Instructions
Have the Assistant provide dosage instructions recomendations. E.g. Provide dosage instructions for ketamine for a 130 kg horse.
Write a Procedure Report for a Patient
Write a full procedure report for patient Baxter, unblocking a cat with FLUTD and obstruction. Sedated with butorphanol, diazepam, and ketamine. Uneventful catheterization with closed end Tomcat and cleared the obstruction with saline flush. Indwelling red rubber catheter placed with closed system and diurese on 2 times maintenance.
Write Discharge Instructions
Write me discharge instructions for Sue Smith who is the owner of Sam for a routine neuter procedure including carprofen and trazodone to go home and a recheck in 7-10 days.
Write a Dental Report
Write me a dental report for a 12 year old spayed female DSH with routine COHAT and extraction of 307 and 407 due to resorptive lesions, closure with 4-0 monocryl. Stable anesthesia.
Write a Radiology Report
Write a full radiology report of a 3 view abdominal radiograph, revealing foreign material in the stomach, excessive gas, and mineralized Renal Pelvises, all else is normal, taken on September 23rd by doctor Shawn, for patient Fluffy owned by Jen Smith.
